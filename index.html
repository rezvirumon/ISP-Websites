About US

A BROADBAND LEADER

A BIG OPPORTUNITY FOR YOUR BUSINESS GROWTH

ICC Communication is a Nationwide Internet Service Provider having a license from the Bangladesh Telecommunications Regulatory Commission (BTRC). This organization is an integral part of the ICC Group. We offer broadband internet and private data communication solutions over fiber optic, wireless, and satellite technology throughout the country. ICC, with its state-of-the-art redundant backbone infrastructure, assures 100% uptime while providing ultimate client satisfaction, no matter how small a client may be. The Network Operation Center (NOC) is staffed 24/7 basis to respond and fix any issues related to connectivity.
